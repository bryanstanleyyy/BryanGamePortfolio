About Me

Hi there! I'm a passionate game developer with minimal experience in creating interactive experiences. My journey into game development started when I was 12, modding my favorite games and learning to code in my spare time.

Today, I hope to specialize in developing action-adventure and role-playing games with a focus on rich storytelling and innovative mechanics. I believe games are one of the most powerful mediums for creating meaningful experiences and connecting people.

When I'm not coding or designing, you can find me exploring open-world games, hiking in nature, or sketching character concepts for my next project.
